ROCKET LEAGUE

Rocket League is a classic. This game has given me so many great memories with all of my friends.

We used to play it back a year or so ago when we all lived together, but then took a quite long break from it.

We all came back to it a few months ago, and it was like we never left. Every game was fun, even though we could all get a little heated at times.

Now, we are on another break; but I assume we will always come back to it eventually.
