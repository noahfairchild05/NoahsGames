DEADLOCK

Deadlock is a new game this year, 2024, that has popped up out of nowhere.

The game is made by Valve, and is still in its early stages of alpha testing.

My friends and I have already poured hundreds of hours into Deadlock, and we always have fun playing.

One thing I don't like about the game, is how long the matches last. I know its normal for MOBAs to last longer, bit nowadays I just don't have the patience.

The game is amazing, but I think I'm burnt out for a while.
